About Foogle - Your personal recipe finder.

We are a group of 7 students from the University of Manchester and have decided to produce a website to help users find out what recipes they can make with the foods in their inventory.

What makes our site special?

  • A large database of recipes and ingredients.
  • A variety of filters to narrow down your search.
  • The ability to filter only recipes that can be made with the your inventory.
